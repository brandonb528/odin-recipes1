Cheeseburgers

An all around classic, the cheeseburge is always a hit when nothing else is doing the trick. A fantastic mixture of evertyhing that makes a sandwich great, the cheeseburger is always a safe bet!

Here's what you'll need to make them:

  • Four 4 Oz. from 1 Ib. pack burger patties
  • 1 tbsp. salt
  • 1 tbsp. pepper
  • 1 tbsp. garlic powder
  • 1 tbsp. onion powder
  • 2 slices american cheese
  • 2 strips of bacon (optional)
  • 4 hamburger buns

Here is how you cook this meal:

  1. Season 1 Ib. pack of ground meat and mix
  2. Press 1 Ib. pack ground meat into four 4 Oz. patties
  3. Place patties on hot cooking surface, leaving on one side for 6 minutes
  4. Flip after 6 mins
  5. Flip again after 6 mins
  6. Ensure internal temp is 165 degrees fahrenheit
  7. Place cheese slices and bacon onto cooking patties, cook until cheese is melted
  8. Place all the patties on the cheeseburger buns and serve
