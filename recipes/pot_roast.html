Pot Roast

If you're looking for a nice, warm, hearty meal on a chilly winter night, pot roast is unmatched.

From it's savory and tender beef, complimented by soft and delicate potatoes, complimented by the sweetness of slow cooked carrots; pot roast is a delicious, yet simple and quick, meal to make at home. Prep time is about 10 mountes.

Here's all you need for this meal:

  • 1 Ib. beef tenderloin
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1/2 cup warm water
  • 8 1/2 size baby potatoes
  • 8 1/2 size carrots
  • 2 sprigs of thyme

Here are steps to cook this meal:

  1. Place your tenderloin into the slow cooker
  2. Add your 1 cup beef broth and water
  3. place your potatoes and carrots into the broth, make sure covered
  4. Add your thyme
  5. Cook on high for 8 hours
  6. Enjoy!
