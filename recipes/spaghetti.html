Spaghetti

A nice home classic meal, spaghetti is one of my all time favorite meals. Today I'll be showing you a recipe that was passed down all the way from my grandma!

First Were going to need our ingredients:

  • 1 Jar Spaghetti sauce
  • 1 tbsp. Rosemary
  • 1/2 tbsp. Thyme
  • 1/2 tbsp. Salt
  • 1/4 tbsp. Pepper
  • 3/4 tbsp. Onion powder
  • 3/4 tbsp. Garlic Powder
  • one 1 Oz. package of spaghetti noodles
  • 8 cups boiling water

Follow below for steps on how to cook this:

  1. Bring 8 Cups of water to a boil
  2. Start bringing spaghetti sauce to a slow boil
  3. Place noodles in rolling boil water for 10-12 minutes
  4. Season sauce with all of your seasonsings as seen above
  5. Once pasta is tender, strain pasta water into sink, return to pot
  6. Grab a plate and get your pasta and sauce, top with grated parmesan cheese for extra flavor!
